Tax logic inspired by the contract

+

FLLA uses a predictable basis-point system to balance burns and treasury growth.

+
+
+
+
+

Early sell guard

+

While timeElapsed < earlySellDuration and earlySellTaxBasisPoints > 0, sells to the contract split the tax in half: 50% burn + 50% owner. All other transfers in that window are untaxed.

+
+
+

Normal phase

+

After the early window, taxRateBasisPoints is applied to every transfer. The same 50/50 split burns a portion of supply while funding operations through the owner wallet.

+
+
+

Burn + owner split

+

BurnPart = tax / 2 reduces circulating supply. OwnerPart = tax - burnPart reinforces liquidity, audits, and community programs, following the contract's transferWithTax rules.
